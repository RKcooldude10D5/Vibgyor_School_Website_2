Achievments

Top Education Brands Award

Mr Rustom Kerawalla, Founder Chairman of VIBGYOR Group of Schools, has established one of the most renowned and strongest network of 40 schools spread across 7 states. He has been the driving force behind the development of hi-tech infrastructure and unique curriculum of VIBGYOR. Under his exemplary leadership, VIBGYOR Group of Schools has grown manifold. We are humbled to announce that he has been awarded "Top Education Brands Award" by BW Education.

Times Education Icons 2019

VIBGYOR Group Of School has been recognised as ‘Times Education Icons 2019’ by Times of India. Our Vice-Chairperson, Ms. Kavita Sahay received the award.

Global League Institute Certificate 2018

We are proud to announce that VIBGYOR High was honoured with the Global League Institute Certificate 2018 by 'Great Place to Study' at the House of Commons, London. Our Founder Chairman, Mr. Rustom Kerawalla and Vice Chairperson, Ms. Kavita Sahay received the award.

Best Education Brand 2018

We are immensely proud to announce that VIBGYOR Group of Schools has been recognised as Best Education Brand 2018 by The Economic Times!

Global Innovative School Award (GISA) 2018

We are proud to announce that VIBGYOR has won the Global Innovative School Award (GISA) 2018, for exemplary performance in education, evaluated through the factors of impact, academic performance, cultural inclusion, social awareness and use of technology!

Extraordinaire Award at the Brand Vision Summit 2017-18

Mr. Rustom Kerawalla, Founder-Chairman of VIBGYOR Group of Schools, has been felicitated for his outstanding contributions to the field of education and has been conferred with the Extraordinaire Award at the Brand Vision Summit 2017-18!
